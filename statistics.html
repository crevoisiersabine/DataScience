Bayesian Analysis of Studying

Bayesian statistical analysis of our working patterns during the 6 months


As a demonstration of the application of bayesian statistical analysis we attempt to answer the question, did our working habits change over the 6 months of studying.

This exercise borrows heavily from analysis to be found in the excellent book Bayesian Analysis for Hackers , so credit where credit is due
